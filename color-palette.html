Color Usage Guidelines

Primary Actions

Use waymo-secondary (#ff6b35) for primary buttons, CTAs, and important actions.

Interactive Elements

Use waymo-accent (#0ea5e9) for links, focus rings, and interactive elements.

Success States

Use waymo-success (#10b981) for completed actions, success messages, and positive feedback.

Error States

Use waymo-danger (#ef4444) for errors, destructive actions, and critical warnings.
